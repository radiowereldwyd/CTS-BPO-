# CTS BPO – 12-Month Financial Projection Model

## 📊 Assumptions

- All customers pay via Ozow.
- AI negotiation win rate: ±90%.
- Operational success rate: ±88–90%.
- Resolution rate for failed contracts: >85%.
- Conservative model: 17 clients in total at launch.
