## Overview

This document describes the complete customer journey from initial acquisition through to reporting and renewal.

---

## Stage Descriptions

### 1. Market Scan
- AI continuously scans global markets for potential clients.
- Predictive scoring ranks prospects by profitability and fit.

### 2. Automated Outreach
- Professional emails sent automatically to identified prospects.
- Follow-up sequences managed by AI.

### 3. Contract Upload & Analysis
- Client uploads contract or submits requirements.
- AI analyzes scope, complexity, and profitability.
- Decision: handle internally or assign to subcontractor.

### 4. Execution & Monitoring
- AI monitors progress in real-time.
- Quality checks performed at each milestone.
- Alerts triggered if performance deviates from targets.

### 5. Payment Flow
- Successful delivery triggers automated Ozow payment.
- Records updated in accounting module.
- Invoice generated and sent to client.

### 6. Reporting
- Dashboard updated with contract outcome.
- Financial reports generated.
- AI self-optimizes based on outcome data.
