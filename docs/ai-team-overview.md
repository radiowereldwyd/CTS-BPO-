# CTS BPO – AI Team Overview

## Overview

CTS BPO is designed so that AI takes over all the core functions of a traditional BPO organization. Each module acts like a team member, but everything is automated and AI-driven. There is no traditional team of people — the AI is the team.

## Module Details

### 🟦 AI Sourcing & Outreach
- **Role**: Find new clients and send professional emails.
- **Function**: Works like your sales team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Daily market scans for new prospects.
  - Automated email sequences.
  - Prospect scoring and prioritization.

### 🟩 AI Negotiation Engine
- **Role**: Negotiate prices with margin protection.
- **Function**: Works as your contracts team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Dynamic pricing based on contract complexity.
  - Margin floor protection (never negotiates below threshold).
  - Win rate target: ±90%.

### 🟨 AI Contract Manager
- **Role**: Receive and analyze contracts, decide on internal handling or outsourcing.
- **Function**: Works like your legal team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Contract parsing and risk assessment.
  - Routing decision (internal vs. subcontractor).
  - SLA tracking and milestone management.

### 🟧 AI Subcontractor Assignment
- **Role**: Select the right subcontractor based on capacity and availability.
- **Function**: Works as your operations team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Real-time capacity matching.
  - Performance history weighting.
  - Automated briefing and onboarding.

### 🟥 AI Payment Gateway (Ozow)
- **Role**: Handles all payments and accounting automatically.
- **Function**: Works like your finance team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Ozow payment initiation on contract completion.
  - Multi-currency tracking (ZAR + USD).
  - Automated invoicing and receipt generation.

### 🟪 AI Audit Trail Logger
- **Role**: Monitor quality, log failed contracts, create transparent reports.
- **Function**: Works as your compliance team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Real-time event logging.
  - Failed contract detection and escalation.
  - POPIA/GDPR/CCPA compliance monitoring.

### ⚪ Dashboard & Reporting
- **Role**: Provides status panels, failed contracts section, and financial reports.
- **Function**: Works like your management team.
- **Key Actions**:
  - Real-time dashboard updates.
  - Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly summaries.
  - AI Initiate button for manual workflow triggers.

## Self-Learning Cycle

The AI improves itself continuously:

1. **Observe** → Collect operational data from all modules.
2. **Analyze** → Identify inefficiencies and opportunities.
3. **Adapt** → Adjust algorithms and pricing models.
4. **Report** → Push performance insights to dashboard.
5. **Reinforce** → Update model weights based on outcomes.
